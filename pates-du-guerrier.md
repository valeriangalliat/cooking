# 🍝 Pâtes du guerrier / pâtes du seigneur

## Ingredients

* Penne pasta
* Beef tomato pasta sauce
* Lardons
* Tuna
* Eggs
* Olive oil
* Cream
* Lots of cheddar
* Lots of Parmesan
* Salt, pepper

## Preparation

Cook pasta.

Add all the things.

Enjoy.

## Variant

* Spaghettini
* Tuna in olive oil (put the oil as well)
* Cream
* Lots of cheddar
* Lots of Parmesan
* Salt, pepper
* Little bit of Sriracha
